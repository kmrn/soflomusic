Chris Montague - Guitar

Born in Maryland, at an early age Chris moved to Pine Island, FL. Chris started playing guitar at age 10 and has been pursuing a career in music ever since. While growing up on Pine Island he was exposed to blues/rock musicians and this influence is easily heard in his playing today. Chris has been teaching Guitar in the Delray Beach area for over 5 years and recently founded SoFLo Music School. He has been a major part of musical projects, including ARTIKaL Sound System, and Stampede Movement, and has also performed with prestigous reggae artists, such as Biggz General, RedLyte, Apple Gabriel and the Abyssinians.
