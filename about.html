Get to Know SoFLo

At SoFLo Music School we provide high quality private music lessons in a comfortable and friendly environment. Our expert staff helps make learning music a fun and fulfilling experience.

Lessons are custom tailored to each student based on their personal goals and current level of playing. All lessons are one-on-one in our private lesson rooms. We offer lessons for Guitar, Piano, Drums, Ukulele, Voice, Bass, Violin, and Woodwinds.

Not only is SoFLo Music School a leading provider in music education, it's also a convenient place to pick up any music accessories you may need. We carry strings, picks, drum sticks and much more. Come by and visit us!
